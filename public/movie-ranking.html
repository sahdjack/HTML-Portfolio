The Top 3 Theater Worthy Movies Of 2023

Movies of 2023 That Are A Must Watch In The Theater (so far..)

Top Gun: Maverick

If there's any movie that should be seen in the theater, it's Top Gun.
The sound, the cinamatography, the sound. This movie is what IMAX and Atmos sound were made for!

Avatar: The Way Of Water

The much anticipated digital masterpiece by James Cameron is finally here in 2023. Visually stunning and immersive, this movie,
is a must watch in the theater.

Super Mario Brothers

I only have about 1-2 theater outtings with my kids in me per year. This year, it was a no brainer that if we only went to one movie this year,
it was Super Mario Brothers. This movie is fun, fast and great for all ages. Fun for the kids and lots of nostalgia for the adults.
They threw every Nintendo character and reference they could at the audience. It was a Nintendo lovers dream. The kids had a blast and so did I!
